About Project

This visual novel is a reference to a family's history. It is called "Generations of Giving". It is currently available on App Store and Play Store. The visual novel has a total of 8 chapters. It also includes games and AR that enhance the story and make it more interactive. During the story, users can interact with the story by selecting the options they want. This could affect the story being shown.

It starts with Koh Choon Joo who is known as CJ. It is about his life storyÂ and he gives back to the community. It also includes how he pass down his legacy of giving to others.

The project is done by a group of people from designers, illustrators, and developers. For my part, I work with another developer to do the visual novel. Everything in the project starts from scratch from the design to the code. He did the basic story mechanics and two games. I enhanced the story mechanic and the games.
